## Data Sources

### BfDI — Bundesbeauftragter für den Datenschutz und die Informationsfreiheit

**URL:** https://www.bfdi.bund.de/  
**Type:** Federal regulatory authority  
**Language:** German (de)  
**License:** Public domain — official German federal publications

**Covered content:**
- Bußgeldbescheide (fine decisions)
- Anordnungen (orders)
- Verfahren (proceedings)
- Bescheide (administrative decisions)

### DSK — Datenschutzkonferenz

**URL:** https://www.datenschutzkonferenz-online.de/  
**Type:** Conference body of German data protection supervisory authorities  
**Language:** German (de)  
**License:** Public domain — official publications

**Covered content:**
- Orientierungshilfen (orientation aids)
- Kurzpapiere (short papers)
- Hinweise (notices)
- Empfehlungen (recommendations)

## Coverage Limitations

- Coverage is limited to publicly available official publications
- Some older decisions may not be indexed
- Data currency: see `de_dp_check_data_freshness` tool
- Private sector and state-level DPA decisions are not included (federal BfDI scope only, plus DSK joint guidance)
